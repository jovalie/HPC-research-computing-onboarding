Hello! This blog is dedicated to high-performance computing (HPC) for research, and maintained by the [Murty Sunak Quantitative and Computing Lab](https://cmc.edu/qcl/) at [Claremont McKenna College](https://cmc.edu/).

This space highlights how researchers, staff, and students can use CMC QCL HPC resources to accelerate discovery!


<br>

You can expect posts on:

- research workflow guidance
- cluster access and environment setup
- job submission and performance best practices
- training, events, and lab announcements

For support, contact qcl@cmc.edu.
